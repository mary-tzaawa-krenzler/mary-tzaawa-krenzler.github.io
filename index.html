I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Macroeconomics at St Hugh's College and an associate member of the Economics Department at the University of Oxford.

I am an applied theorist in the area of macroeconomics. My research focuses on understanding the effects of households with information frictions and non-rational expectations in general equilibrium models, wealth and income heterogeneity and monetary policy.

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Goethe University Frankfurt.
