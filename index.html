I work on the intersection of economics and computing, focusing on the technical and economic basis for AI automation. I'm currently the Associate Director of Epoch AI, an organization I co-founded.

Research

My research focuses on the economics of computing, AI automation, and key trends that will likely shape the development of advanced AI systems.

Media

  • I debated the possibility of explosive economic growth from AI with economist Matt Clancy in Asterisk magazine.
  • My work on how compute, algorithms, and data contribute to advances in AI was featured in the UK Government's report on the capabilities and risks from frontier AI.
  • My work on compute in Machine Learning was the first thing cited in Google's CEO Sundar Pichai's announcement of Bard.
  • My work and datasets on the long-run history of AI feature in Our World In Data's The brief history of artificial intelligence .
  • Time covered my work on the pace of progress in AI and on when AI might outsmart humans.
  • An article I co-authored on data usage in AI was discussed in The Atlantic, New Scientist, and MIT Technology Review.
  • I was on 5 Quick Questions with James Pethokoukis of the American Enterprise Institute.
  • The Economist’s June 2022 cover article on AI featured our work on compute trends in machine learning, as did their coverage on Large Language Models.
  • The Financial Times and VentureBeat covered our work on promoting trustworthy AI development.
  • The University of Cambridge's economics faculty wrote about me and my work on my COVID-19-related research.
