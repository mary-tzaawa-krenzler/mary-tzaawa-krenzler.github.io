I am a Fellow (Post-Doc) in Macroeconomics at St Hugh's College, University of Oxford.

I am an applied theorist in the area of macroeconomics. My research focuses on information frictions and non-rational expectations in general equilibrium models, household heterogeneity and monetary policy.

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Goethe University Frankfurt.
